Niravi Public Charitable Trust

Founded by Aradhana in 2019, Niravi works towards bridging inequities and empowering marginalised communities to thrive and lead change.

Over the years, Niravi has supported underprivileged rural schools with better infrastructure, provided ration and health support to tribal and slum communities and led health awareness and Covid-19 relief efforts that reached over 5 lakh people across 15 cities in India through a network of 250 volunteers.

Going forward, Niravi aims to focus on sustainable rural education transformation — creating lasting impact by strengthening learning environments and community participation.
